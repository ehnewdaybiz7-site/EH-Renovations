Meet Ernest Harston

30+ Years Experience

Dependable, Detail-Oriented, Done Right

Ernest Harston is a project manager, carpenter, painter, electrician, flooring installer, framer, sheetrock specialist, and professional property rehabber with over 30 years of experience doing total restorations and project management for real estate investors across California, Missouri, and Hawaii.

He arrives equipped with compressors, finish and framing nail guns, table and miter saws, commercial-grade painting equipment, and every hand tool needed to handle your project start to finish. Whether it's a complete kitchen gut-reno or a custom backyard shed, Ernest manages and executes every phase personally.

  • Owner-Operated: You work directly with Ernest from the first walk-through to final punch list.
  • Full-Scope Skills: Carpentry, painting, electrical, plumbing, flooring, framing, sheetrock, texture, and wood rot repair.
  • Certified & Equipped: OSHA safety, welding, electronic technology, blueprint reading, and lift truck operation certifications.
What We Do

Our Renovation Services

Kitchen Remodeling

Custom cabinet installation, countertop fitting, backsplash tiling, under-sink plumbing with garbage disposals, sub-floor prep, and complete flooring—carpet, linoleum, laminate, or ceramic tile.

Bathroom Renovations

Concrete slab demolition, underground PVC drain routing, custom shower framing and tiling, pedestal sink and fixture installation, water heater replacement, and full plumbing rough-in to finish.

Decks, Sheds & Exteriors

Custom decks with handrails and lattice skirting, garden sheds built from the ground up (including stilted-base footers for sloped yards), exterior siding, roof sheathing, and complete house painting.

Framing, Drywall & Paint

Structural wall framing, sheetrock hanging, taping, texturing, wood rot repair and replacement, custom stair construction, finish trim carpentry, and full interior/exterior painting with commercial-grade equipment.
